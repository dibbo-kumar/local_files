We've achieved 125K active users in just 18 months, with 45% month-over-month growth. Our platform has processed over $8.5M in transactions from eco-conscious brands and sustainable retailers.

💼 Business Model

GreenTech Solutions operates on a commission-based model:

  • Seller Commission: 3-5% on all transactions (based on volume)
  • Buyer Fees: Micro-premium (0.5%) for express delivery
  • Premium Memberships: $99/month for sellers with advanced analytics
  • API Access: Custom pricing for enterprise integrations

Current MRR breakdown: 70% from seller commissions, 20% from memberships, 10% from other sources.

🎯 Market Opportunity

Total Addressable Market (TAM): $180 Billion in global sustainable goods market

Growth Rate: The sustainable e-commerce market is growing at 20% CAGR, while traditional e-commerce grows at only 8%

Competitive Advantage: We're the first platform combining AI-powered matching (connecting sustainable brands with eco-conscious buyers) with carbon footprint tracking and blockchain-verified sustainability claims.
